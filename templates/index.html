Word Unscrambler is a simple online tool for unscrambling/solving scrambled words, typically useful in solving word game puzzles such as Scrabble, Words with Friends, Wordscraper, TextTwist etc. So, if you need any help (or perhaps, you want to cheat a little) in such word games, this site will help you in that :-) You can enter up to 12 letters (including two wild cards or blank tiles) and it shows you the valid words that can be made from the given scrambled letters.
